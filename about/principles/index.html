2. PrinciplesÂ¶

When designing the MCMI system, we have kept certain quiding principles and frameworks in mind to guide development and architecture over time. Our goals are to encourage cooperative evolution so the software can stay relevant for a long time to come. This way we can build a sizable and diverse repository of open and proprietary extensions taylored for organizations of all shapes and sizes (an integration of popular technologies and processes).

To help modernize infrastructure management we must follow successful modern practices.
